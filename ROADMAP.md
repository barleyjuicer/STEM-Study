\# Roadmap



\## Current Goal



Provide a unified study environment for mathematics, physics, cryptography, and related STEM subjects.



\## Near-Term Improvements



\* Typed-answer questions

\* Formula support

\* Unit conversion tools

\* Problem-solving workspace

\* Topic organization



\## Future Enhancements



\* Physics problem generator

\* Cryptography exercises

\* Mathematical proof practice

\* Formula reference library

\* Graphing and visualization tools



\## Long-Term Vision



Create a reusable STEM learning platform that can support multiple subjects while tracking progress and performance.



\## Research Ideas



\* AI tutoring assistance

\* Personalized learning paths

\* Difficulty adaptation

\* Interactive simulations
